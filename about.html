Tastira

TASTIRA transcends fashion—it is where tradition meets contemporary elegance. Rooted in India's rich cultural heritage, each piece is a tribute to artisanal craftsmanship, timeless fabrics, and bold, modern silhouettes. Every garment tells a story—of identity, creativity, and sophistication—reinterpreting classic elements for today's global fashion landscape.

TASTIRA exemplifies our commitment to innovation, sustainability, and thoughtful design. By blending age-old techniques with modern trends, we create pieces that are not only stylish and versatile but also meaningful and enduring. From the initial concept to the final stitch, every creation is meticulously curated to reflect individuality, confidence, and a conscious approach to fashion.

Our goal is simple yet powerful: to craft designs that resonate with the modern consumer, honoring the past while embracing the future, and to establish a standard of excellence in apparel that is both ethical and aspirational.

Kamlie

Kamlie is a contemporary womenswear brand focused on affordable ethnic and Indo-western styles that seamlessly blend traditional charm with everyday comfort. With breathable fabrics, thoughtful silhouettes, and trendy prints, Kamlie offers a versatile wardrobe for the urban Indian woman. To redefine everyday ethnic and Indowestern wear with timeless co-ord sets that combine comfort, elegance, and conscious craftsmanship for modern Indian women.

Under the GOWROUL umbrella, Every Kamlie creation celebrates individuality and confidence, allowing women to express their style effortlessly while staying comfortable.

Perfect for work, brunch, or evenings out — Kamlie co-ords blend comfort with timeless elegance.
